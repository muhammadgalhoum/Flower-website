About

Our Story

Established in 1986, Flower emerged as Egypt's first floral design house renowned for its flowers and creative displays. With over 30 years of market expertise and a full fledged team working across Egypt,the one-stop design house offers styling for weddings, themed events and corporate functions as well as visual merchandise and artistic flowers for prestigious hotels and fashion brands.

Flower Founder & CEO Carmen Jack's journey into the flower business started 35 years ago. Her passion for flowers has been engraved in her heart and soul since childhood. Her love affair with flowers began when she was a young girl who enjoyed running around in her family's farm and thought that everything touched by nature was simply magical.

Our Mission

Opening more branches and being the best flower shop in the world.

Where we are now

Today, Flower has expanded to three flower stores across Cairo and workshops in Egypt's leading hotels, turning it into a one-stop shop for all our clients' floral and decoration needs. Working with flowers, could also be compared to the art of a poet arranging words to create an exquisite reflection of life. The company strives to provide the best quality flowers, as well as tasteful and aesthetic floral creations. The team is committed to meet and exceed our clients' highest expectations, and is continuously motivated to innovate and create floral magic. The team tackles all projects head-on, from basic floral designs and retail arrangements; to creating what some may call floral sculptures. These fabulous pieces have made Flower's reputation as an enterprise capable of transforming large spaces into breathtakingly beautiful and strikingly dazzling havens.
